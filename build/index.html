Got Questions? We’ve Got Answers!

Get the Information you need to use our tool

How do I enter patient information?

To enter patient information, navigate to the patient input section. Select “New Entry,” and fill in the required fields such as name, age, and relevant medical history. Ensure accuracy before submitting.

What if I don’t see a template that fits my needs?

If you don’t see a suitable template, use the “Custom Template” option. This allows you to tailor the format to meet specific documentation needs and save it for future use.

Is my patient data secure?

Yes, patient data is encrypted and stored in compliance with HIPAA regulations. Security measures ensure only authorized users can access sensitive information, protecting patient privacy.

Can I make changes to the draft?

Yes, edits can be made to the draft by clicking “Edit Draft.” Modify any necessary sections, and the changes will automatically save, ensuring you always have the latest version.

How do I save the document to my EHR system?

To save, select “Export to EHR” from the menu. Choose your EHR system, and the document will sync automatically, making it easily accessible in your healthcare records.
